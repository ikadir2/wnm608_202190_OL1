Color

Savey color branding is built around primary, secondary, and neutral tones to keep the brand consistent and clear. The primary color (#008374) is used for the main brand identity, including the logo and key actions such as CTA buttons. The secondary colors (#FF9743 and #FFC74A) are used for tags and as accent colors in illustrations or highlights.

The neutral light color (#FCFCFC) is used as the main background color. For text, the neutral dark color (#303937) is used for headlines and product titles, while #7B8684 is used for body text.

Cards

The Card is used to highlight important information within a section and to separate content in areas such as the product details, shopping cart, checkout, and the purchase confirmation page. The Card Soft is used for banners and promotional highlights, such as showing how much a user saves or how many points they earn on the purchase confirmation page.

Tags

Tags are used to highlight product details such as expiration dates, discount percentages, and additional information in the product details section. To maintain visual hierarchy, darker colors should be used for the most important information, while lighter colors indicate secondary details.

Navigations

The main navigation is located in the top navigation bar alongside the Savey logo. It allows users to navigate easily between the different pages of the website.


Product Grid

The product grid displays 4 items per row. Each product card is structured to show the price, product name, and a tag indicating the product’s expiration date.


Forms

This input form is used on the cart checkout page and includes fields for name, address, phone number, and credit card details (cardholder name, card number, expiration date, and CCV).


Search Bar

The search bar layout is designed specifically for users to search for products or deals.


Selector

This selector is used for specifying the quantity of items or for selecting options in the filter section.


Buttons

The Dark CTA button is used for standalone call-to-action actions. When there are two CTA buttons to show multiple options, the primary action should use the Dark CTA, while the secondary action uses the Light CTA, creating a clear visual hierarchy in the layout.


Check Boxes

Basic check box are primarily used on the checkout page to allow users to select delivery options. Toggles are used on the product details page to let users subscribe to items.
